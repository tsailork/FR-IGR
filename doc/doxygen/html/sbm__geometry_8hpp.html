Detailed Description

Geometry definitions and ray-casting utilities for the Shifted Boundary Method (SBM).

Defines structures for donor point stencils, surrogate boundary faces, and geometrical diagnostics. Orchestrates the construction of 1D normal rays and Lagrange extrapolation coefficients to support high-order boundary conditions on surrogate Cartesian faces.

Construct a viscous no-slip or moving wall ghost state.

Implements viscous wall boundary conditions by mirroring velocity components and applying temperature/pressure matching rules for adiabatic or isothermal conditions:

  • Adiabatic:

This is primarily utilized in conjunction with the Volume Penalty Method (VPM) or direct face flux calculations for boundary faces.

[in]v_wallPrescribed wall boundary velocity component in the y-direction (m/s)

Detailed Description

1-D Lagrange basis on Gauss-Legendre points for Flux Reconstruction.

Provides the foundational mathematical structures for the high-order Flux Reconstruction (FR) method, specifically the FR-DG (Discontinuous Galerkin) variant.

Includes:

  • Solution-point locations and quadrature weights .
  • Lagrange basis values at cell interfaces: at and at .
  • Derivative matrix .
  • Radau correction polynomial derivatives evaluated at solution points.
